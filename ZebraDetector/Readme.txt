Commands to execute

ZebraDetector 

-video filename, 
./ZebraDetector -video ../input-videos/IMG_0019.avi

-image filename, 
./ZebraDetector -video ../input-files/IMG.JPG

-resizewidth dimension, 
./ZebraDetector -video ../input-videos/IMG_0019.avi -resizewidth 100
